Introduction

I am going to make a website about PUBG for my final project. In this website, I will focus on introducing Mobile PUBG. Although PUBG has become a well know game, mobile PUBG players are not as many as PUBG on PC, which is very regrettable.

I hope that through this project, people who are interested in PBUG will understand that Mobile PUBG is also fun and exciting. At the same time, because Mobile PUBG was developed by a Chinese company called Tencent Technology, I hope to use this website to promote the video game that was made by Tencent Technology.
